What Is SA2?

SA2 means Statistical Area Level 2, an official ABS geography used for regional analysis. It usually represents communities with social and economic interaction.

How To Read The Map

  • Darker/brighter fill means higher opportunity score for selected industry.
  • Score combines unmet business supply and local demand indicators.
  • Use filters to narrow to one state, industry, or suburb search.

Metric Glossary

  • Demand index: 0-1 blend of family composition, family income, and one-year growth.
  • Underserved businesses: expected businesses minus observed businesses (floor at zero).
  • Opportunity score: underserved businesses multiplied by demand factor.
